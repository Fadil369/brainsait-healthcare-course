Module 6: Future Technologies

AI, Blockchain, and the Future of Saudi Healthcare

30 minutes Artificial Intelligence Blockchain

6.1 AI & Predictive Analytics

Artificial Intelligence is transforming healthcare from reactive to proactive. By analyzing vast amounts of data (Genomics, EHR, Wearables), AI can predict disease outbreaks and personalize treatment plans.

Radiology AI

Automating the detection of anomalies in X-rays and MRIs with higher accuracy than humans.

Clinical Decision Support

Alerting doctors to potential drug interactions and suggesting evidence-based treatments.

Predictive Analytics

Forecasting patient admission rates to optimize hospital staffing and resource allocation.

6.2 Blockchain for Data Integrity

Blockchain offers a decentralized, immutable ledger for health data. This is crucial for managing patient consent, securing supply chains (tracking medicine authenticity), and ensuring that medical records cannot be tampered with.

  • Patient Consent Management (Smart Contracts)
  • Drug Supply Chain Integrity
  • Self-Sovereign Identity (SSI)

6.3 Saudi Vision 2030 Alignment

Healthcare Sector Transformation Program

A key pillar of Vision 2030, aiming to restructure the health sector to be comprehensive regarding coverage, effective, and integrated based on the principle of value-based care.

Goal 1

Facilitate access to healthcare services (e-Health)

Goal 2

Digitize health records across the Kingdom (Unified File)
