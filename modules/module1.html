1.1 The Digital Healthcare Revolution

Healthcare organizations today generate massive amounts of data every single day. From electronic health records (EHRs) to medical imaging, laboratory results, wearable devices, and telemedicine platforms‚Äîthe healthcare industry has become one of the most data-intensive sectors globally.

According to recent studies, healthcare data is growing at an unprecedented rate of 36% annually. This exponential growth presents both tremendous opportunities and significant challenges for healthcare providers, payers, and patients alike.

Key Insight

By 2025, healthcare data is expected to reach 36 billion gigabytes globally. To put this in perspective, that's equivalent to approximately 36 billion high-definition movies or 18 billion hours of HD video content.
