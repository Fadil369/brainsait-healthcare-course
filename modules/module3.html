3.1 What is Interoperability?

Healthcare interoperability is the ability of different healthcare information systems, devices, and applications to access, exchange, integrate, and cooperatively use data in a coordinated manner, within and across organizational boundaries, to provide timely and seamless portability of information for optimized patient care.
