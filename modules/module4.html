4.3 Data Residency & Sovereignty in KSA

Compliance is Key

In Saudi Arabia, the National Data Management Office (NDMO) and National Cybersecurity Authority (NCA) enforce strict regulations ensuring that sensitive health data remains within the Kingdom's geographical borders.
